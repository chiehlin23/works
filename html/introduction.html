POPPING

Popping is a style of funk dance and street dance.
It basically uses the technique of rapid muscle contraction and
relaxation to make the dancer's body feel shocked.
This technique is called "pop" or "hit".
With techniques such as "wave" and "isolation",
dancers will use a variety of different movements and poses to
match the rhythm and retake of the song.
Popping dancers are also called "popper".
