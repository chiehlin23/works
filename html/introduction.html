POPPING

Popping is a style of funk dance and street dance.
It basically uses the technique of rapid muscle contraction and
relaxation to make the dancer's body feel shocked.
This technique is called "pop" or "hit".
With techniques such as "wave" and "isolation",
dancers will use a variety of different movements and poses to
match the rhythm and retake of the song.
Popping dancers are also called "popper".

BREAKING

Breakdance is one of the four major elements of hip-hop culture.
The music accompaniment style used began with hip-hop music and rhythm and blues,
and then fed back the development of hip-hop music to this day.
As the most popular type of hip-hop dance,
its influence has evolved from the limited non-mainstream communication
in the United States at the beginning to the wide spread of the world,
becoming a part of modern dance culture.

FreeStyle

Hip-hop is a marginal subculture between African and Latino
youths originating from the South Bronx and Harlem in New York City in the 1970s.
It then developed and expanded into a new art form and swept the world.
Hip-hop includes four elements: rap, DJ, floor break dancing and graffiti.
In addition, subcultures such as rhythmic ventriloquism, hip-hop fashion,
and hip-hop vulgar slang have also emerged.
